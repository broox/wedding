Directions to Derek and Kari's Wedding Ceremony



From I-80:
1. Take the NW 86th St - exit 129
2. Turn right onto NW 86th St and go a little over 1 mile
3. Turn right at NW 62nd Ave (there will be a Kum & Go station to your left before turning)
4. Go about 1/4 mile and take your first right onto Crescent Chase
5. The church will be on your left.
