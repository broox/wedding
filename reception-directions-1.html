Directions to Derek and Kari's Wedding Reception

(Route 1)

  1. Take a right onto NW 62nd Ave and go 1.7 miles
  2. Turn right onto Merle Hay Road and go about 1.5 miles
  3. Turn left onto I-80 East/I-35 North and go about 5.5 miles
  4. Take exit 135 and turn right onto NW 2nd St (2nd St will become 2nd Ave and then 3rd St). Go 4 miles.
  5. Turn right at Grand Ave, go approximately 1/4 mile to the Marriott.

Valet is available at the Hotel (on your left),
otherwise the parking garage will be located on the right.
If you park in the garage, there is a skywalk from the parking garage to the Marriott.
